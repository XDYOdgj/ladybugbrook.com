Gasland MHA18R Propane Heater

The Gasland MHA18R Propane Heater is an ideal choice for bringing warmth to your outdoor or indoor spaces during the colder months. Built with three adjustable heating modes—High, Medium, and Low—it gives you the flexibility to maintain your preferred temperature, whether you're relaxing on a patio, working in a garage, or running a small business.

With a powerful 18,000 BTU output, this heater can comfortably warm up to 450 square feet. Perfect for power outage scenarios or remote setups, this heater is a dependable solution for all your heating needs.
